ğŸ”§ Key Features

1. Login & Home Screen

Quick sign-up/login via Naver and Kakao social logins. The home screen intuitively displays discount banners, popular search terms, and more.

2. Search & Filters

When searching for products, you can filter by various criteria such as price, gender, and discount status. Sorting options include popularity and latest arrivals.

3. Product Details & Size Information

The product details tab provides material, color, and manufacturer information, along with actual size measurements. Image lazy loading is implemented to prevent unnecessary scrolling.

4. Reviews

Reviews can be sorted by rating, latest, photo, or body type. The "See reviews for my size" feature provides personalized information.

5. Recommendations & Inquiries

AI-based recommendations automatically suggest similar products. The inquiry tab allows users to check questions related to delivery, returns, and exchanges.

6. Payment

Before payment, users can double-check quantity, color, and size. Available coupon information is also clearly displayed to prevent mistakes.
