Local Market App

Role: UX Research · Wireframing · Prototyping · Usability Testing

Duration: April 2024 – May 2024

📌 Introduction

This project aims to design a mobile app that transforms the shopping experience for local supermarket customers. By analyzing users’ diverse lifestyles and shopping needs, the app will reduce the inconvenience of grocery shopping through intuitive and efficient features such as real-time discount updates, recipe integration, and personalized supermarket recommendations.

🎯 Project Goals

  • Enhanced customer experience – Stress-free shopping through GPS-based recommendations
  • Increased user engagement – Content integration with discount information and recipes
  • Efficient shopping support – Real-time store information and delivery reservation system

🧠 Design Thinking Process

Ideate → Empathize → Define → Prototype → Test

🔍 Research & Methodology

We conducted in-depth interviews with four individuals (a single-person household, a newlywed couple, a multi-person household, and a stay-at-home mom). The interviews were carried out via face-to-face meetings, phone calls, and messaging. We explored shopping frequency, methods of checking discount information, recipe search habits, and key pain points.

  • How often do you go grocery shopping?
  • Through which channels do you usually shop?
  • How do you find discount information?
  • How do you usually search for recipes?
  • What is the most inconvenient part of grocery shopping for you?

👥 Persona

We analyzed common needs and pain points across different user types based on the interview data.

  • Common Needs
    1. Access to discount information in advance
    2. Purchasing fresh produce
    3. Adapting to digital shopping
  • Common Pain Points
    1. Scattered information
    2. Delivery delays/out-of-stock issues
    3. Lack of connection between recipes and ingredients

💬 Empathy Map

Based on an empathy map that visually organizes users' thoughts, feelings, and behaviors, we identified the key problems the app needs to solve.

🔀 User Flow

App launch → Auto-detect nearby store based on location → View discount information and recipes → Search products and add to cart → Set available delivery time

📝 Wireframe

We visualized the user flow focusing on discount information, recipes, and delivery features, and enhanced screen connectivity and usability through iterative improvements.

🔧 Key Features

1. Login & Home Screen

Various login options provided, including Naver, Kakao, and email. Recently viewed items are displayed on the home screen for quick access without the need to search again.

2. GPS-based Market Setup

Upon agreeing to share location data, the app automatically searches for markets within a 3km radius and provides options to view them on a map or in a list. Users can mark favorites with a star.

3. Today's Special Offers & Recommended Recipes

Suggests special offers from nearby markets along with simple recipes. Recommended ingredients are sorted based on distance and price.

3. Today's Special Offers & Recommended Recipes

Suggests special deals from nearby markets and simple recipes. Recommended ingredients are sorted based on distance and price.

5. Personal Information Screen

Easily manage delivery reservation details, reward points, marketing preferences, and more, all from a single screen.

✅ Conclusion

We designed a commerce platform that goes beyond a simple shopping app, closely aligned with users' lifestyles. Through repeated testing and feedback, we aimed to provide features and experiences tailored to user needs.

  • Efficient and intuitive shopping experience
  • Commerce platform connected to local markets
  • User satisfaction system linking recipes and discount information
